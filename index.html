Featured: Demon Slayer

It's Taisho-era Japan. Tanjiro Kamado is a kindhearted boy whose peaceful life is shattered when a demon slaughters his family. The only survivor is his younger sister, Nezuko, who has been turned into a demon herself.

Vowing to find a cure and avenge his family, Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps, embarking on a perilous journey to hunt down demons and protect humanity, all while carrying his sister on his back.
